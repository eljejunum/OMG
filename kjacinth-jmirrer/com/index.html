Things to do in LaLa-land

Here are a few items we enjoy around town. We think that you'll enjoy them, too.

Fun Stuff

  • Hike Griffith Park to see the back of the famous Hollywood sign
  • Amble over the stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame
  • Take a ¢50-ride on Angels Flight (the “shortest railway in the world”)
  • Encounter the weirdness that is the Museum of Jurassic Technology (seriously, your brain will spin)
  • Say hello to the hippies and kush doctors of the Venice Beach Boardwalk
  • Ride a roller coaster or fly on the trapeze on the Santa Monica Pier
  • View some amazing art (ancient to modern) at LACMA, or check out The La Brea Tar Pits (yes, they still bubble!)
  • Hear a concert or look at the stunning architecture at Disney Hall

Eat

  • Chichen Itza: most scrumptious Yucatan food ever – and our favorite place
  • Colori Kitchen: intimate and contemporary Italian fare (BYOB)
  • Soowon Galbi: traditional Korean BBQ
  • Palms Thai: addicting and spicy (try the curried deer)
  • Bottega Louie: continental European gourmet in a stunningly designed space
  • Más Malo: modern but classic take on LA Chicano-style food
  • Sage: decadent vegan
  • Father’s Office: best burgers and beer

Drink

  • Library Bar: urban chic with books
  • The Standard: sexy rooftop ambiance
  • Wurstküche: the finest German beers paired with exotic sausages
  • The Edison: sip on killer old fashioneds while you watch burlesque
  • The Association: underground speakeasy lounge
  • The Mandrake: hipster watering hole in the Culver City Arts District
  • Down and Out: the only dive bar with a special place in our hearts
