Things to do in LaLa-land

Here are a few items we enjoy around town. We think that you'll enjoy them, too.

Fun Stuff

  • Hike Griffith Park to see the back of the famous Hollywood sign
  • Amble over the stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame
  • Take a 50¢-ride on Angels Flight (the “shortest railway in the world”)
  • Encounter the weirdness that is the Museum of Jurassic Technology (seriously, your brain will spin)
  • Say hello to the hippies and kush doctors of the Venice Beach Boardwalk
  • Ride a roller coaster or fly on the trapeze on the Santa Monica Pier
  • View some amazing art (ancient to modern) at LACMA, or check out The La Brea Tar Pits (yes, they still bubble!)
  • Hear a concert or look at the stunning architecture at Disney Hall

Eat

  • Chichen Itza: most scrumptious Yucatan food ever – and our favorite place
  • Colori Kitchen: intimate and contemporary Italian fare (BYOB)
  • Soowon Galbi: traditional Korean BBQ
  • Palms Thai: addicting and spicy (try the curried deer)
  • Bottega Louie: continental European gourmet in a stunningly designed space
  • Más Malo: modern but classic take on LA Chicano-style food
  • Sage: decadent vegan
  • Father’s Office: best burgers and beer

Drink

  • Library Bar: urban chic with books
  • The Standard: sexy rooftop ambiance
  • Wurstküche: the finest German beers paired with exotic sausages
  • The Edison: sip on killer old fashioneds while you watch burlesque
  • The Association: underground speakeasy lounge
  • The Mandrake: hipster watering hole in the Culver City Arts District
  • Down and Out: the only dive bar with a special place in our hearts

Hotels

We have reserved a room block at the old-school-cool Los Angeles Athletic Club and the very classy Hilton Checkers.

Los Angeles Athletic Club

Established in 1880, the hotel and sports club is located across the street from the Oviatt Penthouse and features access to their extensive athletic facilities, complimentary breakfast, and free WiFi.
If booking your stay, call 213-625-2211 or toll-free at 800-421-8777 and ask for the Jacinth, Mirrer/Wedding rate - $169/night + tax for single room with one bed or $179/night + tax for double rooms with two queen beds.

Hilton Checkers

Formerly the Mayflower Hotel and constructed in the same year as the Oviatt Building (1927), the Checkers is an architectural gem in Downtown Los Angeles with 4-star hotel ammenities.
Booking information will be provided soon. Room rates are $169/night + tax for both single and double rooms.

This is a work in progress so stay tuned for more offerings. We are also working on reserving additional rooms at the Millenium Biltmore.

If you're looking for alternative accommodations, we recommend looking at airbnb. Try staying close to Downtown, though, so you don't need to worry about driving, renting a car, or getting taxis.

Flights

Coordinate your flights to try to get a discount from the airlines. Virgin America generally offers 5% off if you book for 10 or more, but you'll need to select your flights first. Email Groups@virginamerica.com for more information. Other airlines offer similar programs.

The best way to get to and from the airport is on a private shuttle service. It's an even better deal if you travel as a group. Book in advance with Super Shuttle or Prime Time Shuttle.

Parking

All hotels offer overnight parking for guests ($20 at the Athletic Club). There are several other lots in the area that usually cost anywhere from $5 to $10 for the evening, depending on how far you're willing to walk.
